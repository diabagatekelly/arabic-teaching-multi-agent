# Dialogue Completion Template

## Pattern
Complete conversations using appropriate pronouns and nominal sentences.

## Example 1: Introduction Dialogue
**A:** مَرْحَباً! أَنَا طَالِب جَدِيد. ___ طَالِب؟
(Hello! I am a new student. Are you a student?)
**Answer:** أَنْتَ (anta - you, if speaking to male)

**B:** نَعَم، ___ طَالِب. 
(Yes, I am a student.)
**Answer:** أَنَا (ana)

## Example 2: Describing Someone
**A:** مَن هَذَا؟ (Who is this?)
**B:** ___ مُدَرِّس.
**Answer:** هُوَ (huwa - he is a teacher)

**A:** و مَن هَذِهِ؟ (And who is this?)
**B:** ___ طَالِبَة.
**Answer:** هِيَ (hiya - she is a student)

## Example 3: Asking About Objects
**A:** مَا هَذَا؟ (What is this?)
**B:** ___ كِتَاب جَدِيد.
**Answer:** هَذَا (hatha - this is a new book)

**A:** ___ كَبِير؟ (Is it big?)
**B:** نَعَم، ___ كَبِير.
**Answer:** هُوَ، هُوَ (Is it big? Yes, it is big)

## Generation Guidelines
- Use realistic conversational contexts
- Include question-answer patterns
- Practice pronoun usage in context
- Mix vocabulary and grammar naturally
- Keep dialogues short (2-4 exchanges)
- Use Lessons 1-2 content
