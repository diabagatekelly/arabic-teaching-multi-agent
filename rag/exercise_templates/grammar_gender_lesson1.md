# Noun Gender Identification Template

## Pattern
Identify whether the given noun is masculine or feminine.

## Example 1
**Word:** كِتَاب (kitaab - book)
**Gender:** Masculine
**Explanation:** Ends with a regular consonant, no ة marker

## Example 2
**Word:** مَدْرَسَة (madrasa - school)
**Gender:** Feminine
**Explanation:** Ends with ة (taa marbuuta), classic feminine marker

## Example 3
**Word:** قَلَم (qalam - pen)
**Gender:** Masculine
**Explanation:** No feminine markers present

## Example 4
**Word:** طَاوِلَة (taawila - table)
**Gender:** Feminine
**Explanation:** Has ة ending indicating feminine gender

## Generation Guidelines
- Use vocabulary from Lesson 1
- Include mix of masculine and feminine nouns
- Provide clear explanations referencing ة marker
- Show examples with and without the marker
- Help students develop pattern recognition
