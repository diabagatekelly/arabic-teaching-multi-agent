# Subject Pronouns Identification Template

## Pattern
Identify the correct subject pronoun for the given context.

## Example 1
**Context:** Talking about yourself
**Pronoun:** أَنَا (ana - I)
**Example sentence:** أَنَا طَالِب (ana taalib - I am a student)

## Example 2
**Context:** Talking about a male person (he)
**Pronoun:** هُوَ (huwa - he)
**Example sentence:** هُوَ مُدَرِّس (huwa mudarris - he is a teacher)

## Example 3
**Context:** Talking about a female person (she)
**Pronoun:** هِيَ (hiya - she)
**Example sentence:** هِيَ طَالِبَة (hiya taaliba - she is a student)

## Example 4
**Context:** Addressing a male person (you)
**Pronoun:** أَنْتَ (anta - you, masculine)
**Example sentence:** أَنْتَ طَالِب (anta taalib - you are a student)

## Example 5
**Context:** Addressing a female person (you)
**Pronoun:** أَنْتِ (anti - you, feminine)
**Example sentence:** أَنْتِ طَالِبَة (anti taaliba - you are a student)

## Generation Guidelines
- Cover all Lesson 2 pronouns (أَنَا، هُوَ، هِيَ، أَنْتَ، أَنْتِ)
- Show clear context for each pronoun usage
- Include gender distinctions for 2nd person
- Provide simple example sentences
- Use familiar vocabulary from Lessons 1-2
