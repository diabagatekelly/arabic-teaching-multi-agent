# Vocabulary in Context Template

## Pattern
Choose the correct word to complete the sentence based on context.

## Example 1
**Sentence:** ___ يَذْهَبُ إلى المَدْرَسَة (goes to school)
**Options:** 
A) هُوَ (huwa - he) ✓
B) هِيَ (hiya - she)
C) أَنْتَ (anta - you)
**Answer:** A) هُوَ

## Example 2
**Sentence:** ___ طَالِبَة جَدِيدَة (is a new student, feminine)
**Options:**
A) هُوَ (huwa - he)
B) هِيَ (hiya - she) ✓
C) أَنَا (ana - I)
**Answer:** B) هِيَ

## Example 3
**Sentence:** أَنَا ___ (I am...)
**Options:**
A) مُدَرِّس (mudarris - teacher, masculine) ✓
B) مُدَرِّسَة (mudarrisa - teacher, feminine)
C) طَالِب (taalib - student, masculine)
**Answer:** Depends on speaker gender

## Generation Guidelines
- Use Lesson 2 vocabulary (pronouns, basic verbs)
- Provide context clues for gender/number agreement
- Include pronouns and their appropriate contexts
- Show how pronouns work with verbs and nouns
- 3 options per question
