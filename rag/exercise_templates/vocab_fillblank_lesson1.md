# Vocabulary Fill-in-the-Blank Template

## Pattern
Complete the sentence by filling in the blank with the correct Arabic word.

## Example 1
**English:** The ___ is on the table.
**Arabic:** ال___ على الطاولة
**Answer:** كِتَاب (book)
**Transliteration:** al-kitaab 'ala al-taawila

## Example 2
**English:** I went to the ___ today.
**Arabic:** ذَهَبْتُ إلى ال___ اليوم
**Answer:** مَدْرَسَة (school)
**Transliteration:** dhahabtu ila al-madrasa al-yawm

## Example 3
**English:** Where is the ___?
**Arabic:** أَيْنَ ال___؟
**Answer:** بَيْت (house)
**Transliteration:** ayna al-bayt?

## Generation Guidelines
- Use vocabulary from Lesson 1
- Provide context clues in the sentence
- Include transliteration for learner support
- Keep sentences simple and relevant
