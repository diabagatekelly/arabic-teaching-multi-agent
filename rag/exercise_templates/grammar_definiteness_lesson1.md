# Definiteness Identification Template

## Pattern
Identify whether phrases are definite or indefinite and explain why.

## Example 1
**Phrase:** كِتَاب كَبِير (kitaab kabiir)
**Definiteness:** Indefinite
**Explanation:** No ال article, means "a big book"

## Example 2
**Phrase:** الكِتَاب الكَبِير (al-kitaab al-kabiir)
**Definiteness:** Definite
**Explanation:** Both noun and adjective have ال, means "the big book"

## Example 3
**Phrase:** المَدْرَسَة (al-madrasa)
**Definiteness:** Definite
**Explanation:** Has ال article, means "the school"

## Example 4 (Error Example)
**Phrase:** الكِتَاب كَبِير (al-kitaab kabiir)
**Definiteness:** Mixed (Error!)
**Explanation:** Noun is definite but adjective is indefinite - both must match

## Generation Guidelines
- Show definite phrases (ال on noun and adjective)
- Show indefinite phrases (no ال)
- Include error examples where articles don't match
- Explain the meaning difference (the vs. a)
- Use simple Lesson 1 vocabulary
- Emphasize agreement rule: both noun and adjective must match
