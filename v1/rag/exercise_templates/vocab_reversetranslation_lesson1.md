# Reverse Translation Template (Arabic to English)

## Pattern
Translate the given Arabic word or phrase to English.

## Example 1
**Arabic:** كِتَاب
**Transliteration:** kitaab
**English:** book

## Example 2
**Arabic:** المَدْرَسَة
**Transliteration:** al-madrasa
**English:** the school

## Example 3
**Arabic:** بَيْت كَبِير
**Transliteration:** bayt kabiir
**English:** big house / a big house

## Example 4
**Arabic:** الطَالِبَة الجَدِيدَة
**Transliteration:** al-taaliba al-jadiida
**English:** the new student (female)

## Generation Guidelines
- Start with single words
- Progress to words with articles
- Move to simple phrases (noun + adjective)
- Accept multiple valid translations
- Use Lesson 1 vocabulary only
- Include transliteration as pronunciation aid
