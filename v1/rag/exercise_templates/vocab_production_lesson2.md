# Vocabulary Production Template - Lesson 2

## Pattern
Create original sentences using given vocabulary and pronouns.

## Example 1
**Given:** أَنَا (ana - I), طَالِب (taalib - student), جَدِيد (jadiid - new)
**Sample sentences:**
- أَنَا طَالِب (ana taalib - I am a student)
- أَنَا طَالِب جَدِيد (ana taalib jadiid - I am a new student)

## Example 2
**Given:** هُوَ (huwa - he), مُدَرِّس (mudarris - teacher), الكِتَاب (al-kitaab - the book)
**Sample sentences:**
- هُوَ مُدَرِّس (huwa mudarris - He is a teacher)
- الكِتَاب جَدِيد (al-kitaab jadiid - The book is new)
- هُوَ مُدَرِّس جَدِيد (huwa mudarris jadiid - He is a new teacher)

## Example 3
**Given:** هِيَ (hiya - she), المَدْرَسَة (al-madrasa - the school), كَبِيرَة (kabiira - big)
**Sample sentences:**
- هِيَ طَالِبَة (hiya taaliba - She is a student)
- المَدْرَسَة كَبِيرَة (al-madrasa kabiira - The school is big)
- هِيَ مُدَرِّسَة (hiya mudarrisa - She is a teacher)

## Generation Guidelines
- Provide vocabulary prompts (2-4 words)
- Accept multiple valid sentence combinations
- Encourage creativity within grammar rules
- Check for proper agreement (gender, definiteness)
- Use Lesson 1-2 vocabulary
- Focus on nominal sentence structure
