# Themed Vocabulary - Classroom

## Word List

### Objects
1. **كِتَاب** (kitaab) - book
2. **قَلَم** (qalam) - pen
3. **دَفْتَر** (daftar) - notebook
4. **طَاوِلَة** (taawila) - table
5. **كُرْسِي** (kursii) - chair
6. **سَبُّورَة** (sabbuura) - blackboard

### People
7. **مُدَرِّس** (mudarris) - teacher (m)
8. **مُدَرِّسَة** (mudarrisa) - teacher (f)
9. **طَالِب** (taalib) - student (m)
10. **طَالِبَة** (taaliba) - student (f)

### Descriptors
11. **كَبِير** (kabiir) - big
12. **صَغِير** (saghiir) - small
13. **جَدِيد** (jadiid) - new
14. **قَدِيم** (qadiim) - old

## Practice Patterns

### Pattern 1: Simple Phrases
- كِتَاب جَدِيد (kitaab jadiid) - a new book
- قَلَم صَغِير (qalam saghiir) - a small pen
- طَاوِلَة كَبِيرَة (taawila kabiira) - a big table

### Pattern 2: With Definite Article
- الكِتَاب الجَدِيد (al-kitaab al-jadiid) - the new book
- القَلَم الصَغِير (al-qalam al-saghiir) - the small pen
- الطَاوِلَة الكَبِيرَة (al-taawila al-kabiira) - the big table

## Generation Guidelines
- Group vocabulary by semantic theme
- Include gender variations
- Show common combinations
- Practice both definite and indefinite forms
- Build contextual understanding
