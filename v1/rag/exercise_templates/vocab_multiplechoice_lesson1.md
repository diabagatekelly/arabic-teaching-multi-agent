# Vocabulary Multiple Choice Template

## Pattern
Choose the correct Arabic word that matches the English meaning.

## Example 1
**Question:** What is the Arabic word for "book"?
**Options:**
A) مَدْرَسَة (madrasa)
B) كِتَاب (kitaab) ✓
C) بَيْت (bayt)
D) قَلَم (qalam)
