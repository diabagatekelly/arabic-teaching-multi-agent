# Error Correction Template

## Pattern
Identify and correct errors in Arabic phrases or sentences.

## Example 1 (Gender Agreement Error)
**Incorrect:** مَدْرَسَة كَبِير (madrasa kabiir)
**Error:** Adjective doesn't match feminine noun
**Correct:** مَدْرَسَة كَبِيرَة (madrasa kabiira)
**Explanation:** كَبِير needs ة to agree with feminine noun مَدْرَسَة

## Example 2 (Article Error)
**Incorrect:** ال-مَدْرَسَة كَبِيرَة (al-madrasa kabiira) but الجَدِيد (al-jadiid)
**Full phrase:** المَدْرَسَة الكَبِيرَة الجَدِيد
**Error:** Missing ة on second adjective
**Correct:** المَدْرَسَة الكَبِيرَة الجَدِيدَة
**Explanation:** Both adjectives must agree with feminine noun

## Example 3 (Word Order Error)
**Incorrect:** كَبِير بَيْت (kabiir bayt)
**Error:** Adjective before noun (English order)
**Correct:** بَيْت كَبِير (bayt kabiir)
**Explanation:** In Arabic, adjectives follow nouns

## Example 4 (Article Agreement Error)
**Incorrect:** الكِتَاب كَبِير (al-kitaab kabiir)
**Error:** Adjective needs article when noun has it
**Correct:** الكِتَاب الكَبِير (al-kitaab al-kabiir)
**Explanation:** Definite noun requires definite adjective

## Generation Guidelines
- Focus on common learner errors
- Target grammar points from Lesson 1
- Provide clear explanations
- Show both incorrect and correct versions
- Help students develop error awareness
