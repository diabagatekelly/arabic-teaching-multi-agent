# Error Correction Template - Lesson 2

## Pattern
Identify and correct errors in sentences with pronouns and nominal sentences.

## Example 1 (Pronoun Gender Mismatch)
**Incorrect:** هُوَ طَالِبَة (huwa taaliba)
**Error:** Masculine pronoun with feminine noun
**Correct:** هِيَ طَالِبَة (hiya taaliba) OR هُوَ طَالِب (huwa taalib)
**Explanation:** Pronoun gender must match the noun

## Example 2 (Missing Gender Agreement)
**Incorrect:** أَنْتِ مُدَرِّس (anti mudarris)
**Error:** Feminine pronoun with masculine noun
**Correct:** أَنْتِ مُدَرِّسَة (anti mudarrisa)
**Explanation:** "You (feminine)" requires feminine form of teacher

## Example 3 (Added Verb Error)
**Incorrect:** أَنَا أَكُونُ طَالِب (ana akuun taalib)
**Error:** Added unnecessary verb "to be"
**Correct:** أَنَا طَالِب (ana taalib)
**Explanation:** Arabic nominal sentences don't need "to be" verb

## Example 4 (Wrong Pronoun Choice)
**Incorrect:** هُوَ (pointing to self)
**Error:** Using third person for first person
**Correct:** أَنَا
**Explanation:** Use أَنَا when referring to yourself

## Generation Guidelines
- Focus on pronoun usage errors
- Target nominal sentence structure
- Address gender agreement issues
- Show verb "to be" over-application errors
- Use Lesson 2 grammar and vocabulary
