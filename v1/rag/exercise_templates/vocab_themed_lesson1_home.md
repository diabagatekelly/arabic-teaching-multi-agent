# Themed Vocabulary - Home & Family

## Word List

### Places
1. **بَيْت** (bayt) - house
2. **غُرْفَة** (ghurfa) - room
3. **مَطْبَخ** (matbakh) - kitchen
4. **حَمَّام** (hammaam) - bathroom

### Family
5. **أَب** (ab) - father
6. **أُم** (umm) - mother
7. **أَخ** (akh) - brother
8. **أُخْت** (ukht) - sister

### Objects
9. **بَاب** (baab) - door
10. **شُبَّاك** (shibbaak) - window
11. **سَرِير** (sariir) - bed
12. **مِكْتَب** (maktab) - desk/office

### Descriptors
13. **جَمِيل** (jamiil) - beautiful
14. **نَظِيف** (nadhiif) - clean
15. **وَاسِع** (waasi') - spacious

## Practice Patterns

### Pattern 1: Simple Phrases
- بَيْت جَمِيل (bayt jamiil) - a beautiful house
- غُرْفَة كَبِيرَة (ghurfa kabiira) - a big room
- مَطْبَخ نَظِيف (matbakh nadhiif) - a clean kitchen

### Pattern 2: With Definite Article
- البَيْت الجَمِيل (al-bayt al-jamiil) - the beautiful house
- الغُرْفَة الكَبِيرَة (al-ghurfa al-kabiira) - the big room

## Generation Guidelines
- Focus on home and family vocabulary
- Show real-life usage contexts
- Include both masculine and feminine forms
- Practice adjective agreement
- Build practical communication skills
