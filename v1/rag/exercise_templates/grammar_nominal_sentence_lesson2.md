# Nominal Sentence Formation Template

## Pattern
Create simple nominal sentences (subject + predicate, no verb "to be").

## Example 1
**Subject:** أَنَا (ana - I)
**Predicate:** طَالِب (taalib - student)
**Sentence:** أَنَا طَالِب (ana taalib - I am a student)

## Example 2
**Subject:** هُوَ (huwa - he)
**Predicate:** مُدَرِّس (mudarris - teacher)
**Sentence:** هُوَ مُدَرِّس (huwa mudarris - He is a teacher)

## Example 3
**Subject:** الكِتَاب (al-kitaab - the book)
**Predicate:** جَدِيد (jadiid - new)
**Sentence:** الكِتَاب جَدِيد (al-kitaab jadiid - The book is new)

## Example 4
**Subject:** المَدْرَسَة (al-madrasa - the school)
**Predicate:** كَبِيرَة (kabiira - big, feminine)
**Sentence:** المَدْرَسَة كَبِيرَة (al-madrasa kabiira - The school is big)

## Generation Guidelines
- Emphasize no verb needed (unlike English)
- Show pronoun + noun patterns
- Show definite noun + adjective patterns
- Ensure gender agreement in predicates
- Keep sentences simple and clear
- Build on Lesson 1-2 vocabulary
