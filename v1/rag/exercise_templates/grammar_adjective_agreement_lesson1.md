# Adjective Agreement Practice Template

## Pattern
Match adjectives to nouns with correct gender agreement and form phrases.

## Example 1
**Noun:** كِتَاب (kitaab - book, masculine)
**Adjective:** كَبِير (kabiir - big)
**Phrase:** كِتَاب كَبِير (kitaab kabiir - a big book)

## Example 2
**Noun:** مَدْرَسَة (madrasa - school, feminine)
**Adjective:** كَبِير → كَبِيرَة (kabiir → kabiira - big)
**Phrase:** مَدْرَسَة كَبِيرَة (madrasa kabiira - a big school)

## Example 3
**Noun:** بَيْت (bayt - house, masculine)
**Adjective:** جَمِيل (jamiil - beautiful)
**Phrase:** بَيْت جَمِيل (bayt jamiil - a beautiful house)

## Example 4
**Noun:** طَاوِلَة (taawila - table, feminine)
**Adjective:** جَدِيد → جَدِيدَة (jadiid → jadiida - new)
**Phrase:** طَاوِلَة جَدِيدَة (taawila jadiida - a new table)

## Generation Guidelines
- Show gender agreement with ة addition for feminine
- Include both masculine and feminine noun examples
- Keep adjective vocabulary limited to Lesson 1
- Emphasize adjective follows noun in Arabic
- Provide transliteration for pronunciation support
