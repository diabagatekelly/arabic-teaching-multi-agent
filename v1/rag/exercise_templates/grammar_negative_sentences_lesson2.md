# Negative Sentence Formation Template

## Pattern
Make sentences negative using لَيْسَ (laysa - is not) or لَا (laa - not/no).

## Example 1: Simple Negation with لَا
**Positive:** أَنَا طَالِب (ana taalib - I am a student)
**Negative:** لَا، أَنَا لَسْتُ طَالِب (laa, ana lastu taalib - No, I am not a student)
**Or:** أَنَا لَسْتُ طَالِباً (with accusative case - advanced)

## Example 2: Negating Identity
**Positive:** هُوَ مُدَرِّس (huwa mudarris - He is a teacher)
**Negative:** هُوَ لَيْسَ مُدَرِّس (huwa laysa mudarris - He is not a teacher)

## Example 3: Negating Description
**Positive:** الكِتَاب كَبِير (al-kitaab kabiir - The book is big)
**Negative:** الكِتَاب لَيْسَ كَبِيراً (al-kitaab laysa kabiiran - The book is not big)

## Example 4: Negative Questions
**Question:** هَلْ أَنْتَ طَالِب؟ (hal anta taalib? - Are you a student?)
**Negative answer:** لَا، لَسْتُ طَالِباً (laa, lastu taaliban - No, I am not a student)

## Generation Guidelines
- Introduce لَيْسَ and لَا for negation
- Keep to simple patterns for beginners
- Show question-negative answer pairs
- Use Lesson 2 vocabulary
- Note: Full conjugation of لَيْسَ is advanced - focus on basic forms
