# Definite Article (ال) Practice Template

## Pattern
Add or remove the definite article ال (al-) to make nouns definite or indefinite.

## Example 1
**Given:** كِتَاب (kitaab - a book)
**Add ال:** الكِتَاب (al-kitaab - the book)

## Example 2
**Given:** مَدْرَسَة (madrasa - a school)
**Add ال:** المَدْرَسَة (al-madrasa - the school)

## Example 3
**Given:** الطَالِب (al-taalib - the student)
**Remove ال:** طَالِب (taalib - a student)

## Example 4
**Given:** البَيْت (al-bayt - the house)
**Remove ال:** بَيْت (bayt - a house)

## Generation Guidelines
- Use familiar Lesson 1 vocabulary
- Include both directions (add/remove article)
- Explain meaning change (definite vs indefinite)
- Show how ال attaches directly to the noun
- Practice both masculine and feminine nouns
