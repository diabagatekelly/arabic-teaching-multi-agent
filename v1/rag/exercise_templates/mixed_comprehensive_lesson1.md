# Comprehensive Exercise Template - Lesson 1

## Pattern
Multi-step exercises combining vocabulary, gender, articles, and adjective agreement.

## Example 1: Build a Complete Phrase
**Step 1:** Choose a noun: مَدْرَسَة (madrasa - school)
**Step 2:** Identify gender: Feminine (has ة)
**Step 3:** Choose adjective: كَبِير (kabiir - big)
**Step 4:** Make adjective agree: كَبِيرَة (kabiira - adds ة for feminine)
**Step 5:** Form phrase: مَدْرَسَة كَبِيرَة (a big school)
**Step 6:** Make definite: المَدْرَسَة الكَبِيرَة (the big school)

## Example 2: Analyze a Phrase
**Given:** الكِتَاب الجَدِيد القَدِيم
**Analysis:**
- Noun: كِتَاب (book)
- Gender: Masculine (no ة)
- Definiteness: Definite (has ال)
- Adjectives: جَدِيد (new), قَدِيم (old)
- Agreement: Both adjectives are masculine (no ة), both have ال
- Translation: "the new old book" (contradictory but grammatically correct)

## Example 3: Translation Challenge
**English:** "the small beautiful house"
**Process:**
1. Main noun: house = بَيْت (masculine)
2. Make definite: البَيْت
3. Adjective 1: small = صَغِير
4. Adjective 2: beautiful = جَمِيل
5. Make both definite: الصَغِير، الجَمِيل
6. Final: البَيْت الصَغِير الجَمِيل

## Example 4: Error Detection & Correction
**Sentence:** المَدْرَسَة كَبِير الجَدِيد
**Errors:**
1. كَبِير should be كَبِيرَة (feminine agreement)
2. الجَدِيد should be الجَدِيدَة (feminine agreement)
**Corrected:** المَدْرَسَة الكَبِيرَة الجَدِيدَة

## Generation Guidelines
- Combine multiple grammar concepts
- Include step-by-step problem solving
- Require analysis and application
- Mix all Lesson 1 topics
- Provide detailed explanations
