# Vocabulary Translation Template

## Pattern
Translate the given word or phrase from English to Arabic.

## Example 1
**English:** book
**Arabic:** كِتَاب
**Transliteration:** kitaab

## Example 2
**English:** school
**Arabic:** مَدْرَسَة
**Transliteration:** madrasa

## Example 3
**English:** new house
**Arabic:** بَيْت جَدِيد
**Transliteration:** bayt jadiid

## Example 4 (Phrase)
**English:** the big book
**Arabic:** الكِتَاب الكَبِير
**Transliteration:** al-kitaab al-kabiir

## Generation Guidelines
- Start with single words from Lesson 1
- Progress to simple two-word phrases
- Include both isolated words and words with definite article
- For phrases, use noun + adjective patterns
- Always provide transliteration
