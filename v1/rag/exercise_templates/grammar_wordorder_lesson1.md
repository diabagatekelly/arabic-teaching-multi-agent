# Word Order Practice Template

## Pattern
Arrange words in correct Arabic word order (Noun-Adjective, not Adjective-Noun).

## Example 1
**English order:** big book
**Arabic word order:** كِتَاب كَبِير (kitaab kabiir)
**Rule:** Noun first, then adjective

## Example 2
**Scrambled:** جَدِيدَة / مَدْرَسَة
**Correct order:** مَدْرَسَة جَدِيدَة (madrasa jadiida)
**Translation:** a new school

## Example 3
**Scrambled:** الكَبِير / الكِتَاب / الجَدِيد
**Correct order:** الكِتَاب الكَبِير الجَدِيد (al-kitaab al-kabiir al-jadiid)
**Translation:** the big new book

## Example 4
**English:** the beautiful new house
**Step 1:** Identify parts - house (بَيْت), beautiful (جَمِيل), new (جَدِيد)
**Step 2:** Add article to all - البَيْت، الجَمِيل، الجَدِيد
**Correct order:** البَيْت الجَمِيل الجَدِيد
**Translation:** al-bayt al-jamiil al-jadiid

## Generation Guidelines
- Emphasize noun-adjective order
- Show multiple adjectives (noun first, then all adjectives)
- Include both definite and indefinite phrases
- Give scrambled words for students to arrange
- Use Lesson 1 vocabulary
- Contrast with English word order to highlight difference
